        🚀 How to play

        • Asteroids fall carrying a Japanese term. Type its answer — the romaji reading or English meaning — to blast it.
        • No Enter needed. It fires the instant your text matches. Your ship slides to track the closest asteroid and shoots a beam at it.
        • Overlapping answers are handled. If a short answer is also the start of a longer one (e.g. ん = "n" while typing な = "na"), the short one is destroyed but your text is kept so you can finish the longer word.
        • ❤️ Defend your ship. Don't let asteroids reach the bottom — each one costs a life. On harder modes, wrong answers cost a life too.
        • 🔥 Build a streak. Consecutive hits multiply your score. Each streak milestone (5, 10, 25, 50, 100) doubles points and earns a life — once each, up to your mode's max.
