Write 川

Pass 1: trace the faint guide to learn the strokes. Pass 2: the guide disappears — write it from memory and you're graded on direction, stroke order, count, the spacing/balance between strokes, and fluidity — whether each line is a quick, smooth, direct brush stroke (slow, hesitant strokes are docked). All measured against the real KanjiVG stroke data, no AI needed. You need at least a C (70%) — plus the lesson's knowledge check — to collect the kanji and continue in Kanji Road.
